Content: Jesse James Garrett (2nd co-founder of Adaptive Path), helped popularize the concept of UX with his book Elements of User Experience: User-Centered Design for the Web and Beyond. It redefines and adapts it for web professionals. It is structured around five planes: the surface, the framework, the structure, the scope and the strategy.
